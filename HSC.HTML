WELCOME TO MAYEMEN ANNEX HOSPITAL

MAYEMEN Hospital IN KUMBA since its inception in 1984 one of the pioneer private Hospital in Kumba and as a matter fact all over Southwest Region in Cameroon. The Hospital is situated at the heart of Kumba precisely in Buea Road Kumba. From day one till today it has not compromised on quality services and emerged as one of the best hospitals in public sector. At MAYEMEN Hospital, patient can have access to over 100 consultant doctors from 25 sub-specialities. Our dedicated team of health care professionals is committed to give each patient the best care. Keeping in mind the motto ‘In Life, We care’. Mayemen Hospital is serving for the last 32 years, providing both outdoor and indoor diagnostic services. We try to give the highest priority to the best possible medical care for our patient.






Our Mission



  • We aspire to be the finest Medical facility in the Bangladesh, meeting all the needs of our patients who rely on us for standard healthcare services.

  • We strive to be a facility that brings healthcare of high standards at feasible rates in the Bangladesh.

  • Our team aims to create a new wave with our ability to go beyond the pinnacle of expectations set by our patients.
